Figure 2: This bargraph is displaying the top 10 (more if the words are tied in value) words used in all of the statements, separated as being either a regular word, or a stop word. A stopword refers to the most common words in a language (the, and, is, etc), which were removed using the stop_words variable from the TidyText package in R, with the manual addition of "we're". The regular words are the top used words that are not stop words.
